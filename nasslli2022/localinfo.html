Where can I go out to eat or get a drink?

Downtown LA is only 3 metro stops away on the Expo Line, and uncountably many options. There are also some great options nearby:

  • USC Village has several dining options, coffee shops and a Trader Joe's.
  • Mercado La Paloma, a 20 minute walk away, is a food court with several dining options.
  • Rock and Reilly's is a good place to get a drink nearby.

What museums can I go to?

USC is across the street from Exposition Park (an old World Fair site!), which holds several of LA's museums:

  • Natural History Museum
  • California African American Museum
  • California Science Center
  • Exposition Rose Garden

What else can I do?

  • Go to DoLa to find a vast amount of options for what to do in the city.
  • If you are interested in nature, Griffith Park has hiking trails with nice views and an observatory.
  • If you want to go to the beach, you can travel by the Expo line metro to downtown Santa Monica in 40 minutes.
